About
Charlie Bailey

Greetings. I'm Charlie Bailey, an incoming Cybersecurity student at Kennesaw State University. I built ChuggGPT as a self-directed side project — not for a class, not for a grade, but because application development and AI integration is the kind of thing I do for fun.

The goal was to go beyond tutorials and build something real with genuine moving parts, all wired together from scratch.

  • AI Triage Every ticket is instantly classified — severity, category, business impact, and escalation flags assigned before a human ever sees it.
  • Streaming Chat Agent An AI agent opens a live conversation on every ticket, streaming responses in real time over WebSocket and resolving common issues without human intervention.
  • Automation Suggested runbooks and scripts surface automatically. Admins can trigger safe diagnostics in one click directly from the dashboard.
  • Admin Dashboard Full ticket queue with filters, status controls, audit logs, and agent assignment. Everything in one place, no tab-switching required.
